Agent Actions

All the agents that have defined the class TankAgArch as architecture class will have the actions available:

  • ahread(Qty)

    Move tank ahead.

    Arguments:

    1. Integer Qty - Qty meters to move ahead

  • back(Qty)

    Move tank back.

    Arguments:

    1. Integer Qty - Qty meters to move back

  • dropAccelerator

    Drop the acceleration.

  • turnRight(Angle, Direction)

    Move tank to right.

    Arguments:

    1. Integer Angle - Angle in degrees to turn right.
    2. Integer Direction - 1 to turn right moving ahead, -1 to turn right moving back.

  • turnLeft(Angle, Direction)

    Move tank to left.

    Arguments:

    1. Integer Angle - Angle in degrees to turn left.
    2. Integer Direction - 1 to turn left moving ahead, -1 to turn left moving back.

  • stopTurning

    Stop turning the tank.

  • turnMainGunRight(Angle)

    Turn main gun to right.

    Arguments:

    1. Integer Angle - Angle in degrees to turn right.

  • turnMainGunLeft(Angle)

    Turn main gun to left.

    Arguments:

    1. Integer Angle - Angle in degrees to turn left.

  • stopMainGunTurning

    Stop turning main gun.

  • fireMainGun

    Fire main gun bullet.

  • fireMachineGun

    Fire machine gun bullet.
